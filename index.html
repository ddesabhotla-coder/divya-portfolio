Hi! I am Divya, UX Designer currently based in San Jose, CA. I'm a developer-turned-designer with a passion for problem-solving through both code and design. My Design Values

01 Solve, Do Not Bandaid

Emphasizing that addressing issues thoroughly and thoughtfully is ultimately more efficient than relying on quick fixes, which may accumulate UX debt over time

02 Balancing All Needs

Creating product designs that align with the needs of three essential entities: your company, the business clients you sell products to, and the end users who interact with the product.

03 Autonomy in Design

Empowering users to make informed decisions by providing assistance and guidance to users in a way that doesn't disrupt their natural workflow or force them into decisions.
